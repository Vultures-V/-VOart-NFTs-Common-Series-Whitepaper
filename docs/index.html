VOart NFTs Common Series – Technical Rarity Whitepaper

VOart (Vultures Origami Art) Common Collection blends ancient origami tradition with NFT technology, consisting of 56 digital artworks. This technical review outlines the rarity system and focuses on folding techniques, aura, colors, wings, and vision levels.

1. Introduction: Convergence of Art and Technology

1.1 Birth of VOart Common

VOart (Vultures Origami Art) seamlessly merges ancient Japanese origami with cutting-edge Web3 technology. The series is divided into three tiers defined by folding complexity and rarity: Elite, Refined, and Standard. Each NFT represents a vulture figure crafted with mathematical precision and digital creativity, where the folding technique is the primary driver of rarity and value.

1.2 Roots and Meaning of the Collection Name

The name VOart is an abbreviation of “Vultures Origami Art” and carries deep symbolic meaning:

  • Transformation & Purification: Vultures, nature’s scavengers, symbolize the end of the old and the birth of the new.
  • Rebirth: A symbol of the life cycle following death.
  • High Perspective: A wise, all-seeing view from the sky.

1.3 Philosophical Foundation

“Every fold carries a soul. Every soul is shaped with mastery.” — VOart Manifesto

In the VOart Common series, each fold is a conscious act of creation, symbolizing rebirth, patience, and the freedom of the sky through the vulture motif. Rooted in the rich tradition of Japanese origami, these techniques are reinterpreted as digital assets for collectors, reflecting artistic purpose and technical mastery.

1.4 Japanese Origami Heritage

Origami, meaning “folded paper” in Japanese, emerged over a thousand years ago during the Heian period (794-1185) and evolved from ceremonial uses into a refined art form. Based on Zen principles of precision and simplicity, it transforms a single sheet without cutting or glue. The VOart Common series preserves this cultural legacy, reinterpreting traditional techniques as NFTs while honoring the vulture’s symbolism of patience and rebirth.

2. Technical Rarity System & Features

2.1 Rarity Algorithm

Rarity Score (RS) = TDC × AS × CV × WH × VI
  • TDC (1–5): Folding difficulty (e.g., Sink Fold = 5, Valley Fold = 1)
  • AS (1–5): Aura power (e.g., Annihilator = 5, Gentle = 1)
  • CV (1–5): Color vibrancy (e.g., Snow White = 4, Dust = 1)
  • WH (1–5): Wing harmony (e.g., Angled Wing = 4, Plain Wing = 1)
  • VI: Determined by the Vision Level System (below)

In the Common Series, RS ranges from 50 % (rarest) to 100 % (most common). The most difficult technique, Sink Fold (Vwhite1, 50 %), is the rarest; the simplest, Valley Fold (Vdesert4, 100 %), is the most common.
